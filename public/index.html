Clyde Diary

Take care of your Clyde like never before.

During our college career, we decided to adopt Clyde from the shelter. Given that we were students juggling classes, extracurriculars and work it was difficult for us manage Clyde. Throughout the day, there would be frequent calls and messages asking "Did you take Clyde out?", "Did you give him food/water?", "Did you remember to give him his heartworm medicine?"

Introducing Clyde Diary

Made with ❤️ in Austin, TX. Made in dedication to Clyde by Saras Paudel and Robin Dhakal.
